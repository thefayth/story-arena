# Contributing

Story Arena is not accepting public source-code contributions through this repository.

This repository exists to document the public-facing project surface while the private engine remains protected.

Useful forms of future collaboration may include:

- Accessibility review
- Privacy review
- Public copy review
- Visual asset review
- Partnership conversations
- Screenwriter-centered workflow feedback

Do not submit private scripts, credentials, legal documents, medical records, benefits records, family information, or sensitive screenshots through this repository.
