# Story Arena

Private screenplay vault. Public title-board power. Creator-owned AI feedback and protection workflows.

![Story Arena hero](../assets/hero/hero-image.png)

## What This Project Is

Story Arena is a private-first AI screenwriting platform and public project surface for writers who want support without giving away unfinished work.

## Why It Matters

Screenwriters need notes, visibility, evidence receipts, and protection workflows. They also need control. Story Arena treats private drafts as protected creative assets and public pages as opt-in outputs.

## How It Works

Writers upload or describe work privately. The system creates sanitized script-genome metadata, AI feedback, IP-prep checklists, and visual briefs. The writer reviews what can become public. Only approved summaries, posters, title pages, and awards data leave the private vault.
