# Brand Style Notes

## Direction

Golden pop-cinema writer vault with database clarity.

## Keywords

Bright, protective, polished, theatrical, creator-owned, public-safe, warm, strategic.

## Palette

- Champagne ivory: `#fff8e8`
- Soft paper: `#fffdf4`
- Warm gold: `#d6a334`
- Spotlight gold: `#fad668`
- Cinema black: `#141210`
- Deep plum accent: `#5c4b96`
- Rose accent: `#ab4858`
- Teal accent: `#2a8784`

## Typography

Use strong editorial headings, clean database labels, and plain-language privacy copy.

## Asset Rules

- Use golden Story Arena assets first.
- Retire older dark anime assets from public use.
- Avoid generic SaaS gradients.
- Avoid copying specific artists, film frames, idol groups, album designs, or copyrighted characters.
- Keep Black women and diverse creators visible as authors, judges, technical builders, and protagonists in future generated visuals.
