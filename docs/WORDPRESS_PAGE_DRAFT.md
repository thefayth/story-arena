## What This Project Is

Story Arena is a screenwriter-first AI platform concept that keeps creative work private while helping writers generate feedback, protection receipts, and approved public title pages.

## Why It Matters

Writers should not have to expose unfinished scripts to prove that their work exists. Story Arena is built around privacy, review gates, evidence receipts, and public release by consent.

## How It Works

1. A writer starts inside the private vault.
2. The system creates sanitized script-genome metadata.
3. AI judges provide notes and creative feedback.
4. IP prep tools organize copyright, trademark, patent-triage, and evidence tasks.
5. The writer decides what, if anything, becomes public.
6. Approved titles can appear on public boards, award pages, and social visuals.
